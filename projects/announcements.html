Name-giver’s Compendium: Open Call for Writers [Web Site Announcements]
Posted on February 8, 2006 7:46 PM by Dammi
Just a week to go - the deadline ends on February 14th!

For the forthcoming Name-givers Compendium, we intend to revise and expand the racial Disciplines -- covering all of them in more detail and with better mechanics. While the game mechanics are currently in development, we need some in-game fiction explaining each Discipline's concepts and unique views. We are looking for fictional accounts written from the point of view of followers of the Disciplines of the Name-giver's Compendium.

For more information on the details of this open call (including how to participate), click here!

State of Play: Mid-January [Web Site Announcements]
Posted on January 19, 2006 4:54 PM by Anunnaki
Hi everyone. Well, it's mid-January and here's a brief state of the nation.

The hardcover books are still weeks away. Check my blog out for a rundown on how things are working out. At the moment, given the print defects on the last proofs, I'm just being very cautious that we get these right. Unfortunately, it takes weeks of turnaround time to get things from the US to New Zealand, and tweaks back. I still can't commit to a release date (sorry). So we've gone to plan B. Which is softcover books.

Formal announcement to come, but Richard and I have been working on getting these together this week. Turns out we have a local POD supplier in New Zealand, which is just brilliant. Plus they're really keen to work with us going ahead and have been more than helpful so far. Proofs are being printed tomorrow (Friday) and, if they come out perfectly first time around, we may have softcovers available as soon as next week. Needless to say, while disappointing we couldn't get hardcovers out first, we intended to release softcovers anyway with a lower entry price. Just how plans work out. Once we get these sorted, a formal announcement will be made. You will be able to purchase these from the RedBrick shop.

Miniatures are going well. Richard is still looking to get custom miniatures sculpted in time, but no ETA on that. We're going to be looking at merchandise again soon; certainly broadening what's currently available. OOP FASA eBooks are going well. We're getting those out as quickly as time allows. Same thing with Free Stuff. You can expect more cool goodies over the next few months.

Ardanyan's Revenge is in layout at the moment. We're still gunning for a late January PDF release (February for print edition), and at the moment that's running on track. Likewise, the Name-giver's Compendium is being worked on at present. Thanks to those who have sent in submissions for opening fiction on the additional Disciplines.

Thanks for your patience last year. This is still a big learning curve for all of us and, while we are finding our "sea-legs" now, we really couldn't have done half of what we've done to date without your support and encouragement. 2006 is going to be an interesting year!

Kind regards, JAMES (TEAM REDBRICK)
